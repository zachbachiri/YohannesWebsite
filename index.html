Who is Yohannes?

Yohannes is a group of four optimistic multi-instrumentalists who hope to use their music to make the world a better, more equal place. Named after their Ethiopian maintenance man, Yohannes was born in Boston in 2014. Band members Alex, Eli, Keenan and Zach bring their wide-ranging, conscious and subconscious influences from folk, rock, electronic, hip-hop, and jazz together to create a sound that is truly original.
